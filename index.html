Ring-Road Traffic Simulation

Cars drive around a circular track using the Intelligent Driver Model (IDM) of Martin Treiber and co-authors. Even without obstacles, small perturbations grow into stop-and-go waves — the classic Sugiyama experiment.

Enable the Driver noise slider to add stochastic acceleration error and compare three stochastic car-following models: white noise (B-IDM), a Gaussian-process kernel from Zhang & Sun (2024), MA-IDM, and an autoregressive process from Zhang, Wang & Sun (2024), dynamic-regression IDM.
