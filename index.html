Ring-Road Traffic Simulation

Cars drive around a circular track using the Intelligent Driver Model (IDM). Even without obstacles, small perturbations grow into stop-and-go waves — the classic Sugiyama experiment. Enable the Driver noise slider to add stochastic acceleration error — compare white noise (B-IDM), an RBF GP (MA-IDM), and an AR(1) process (dynamic regression IDM).
